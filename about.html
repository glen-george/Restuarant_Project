About Us

-Malabar Café a modern South Indian restaurant dedicated to the authentic culinary and cultural heritage of Kerala.

Key Features & Highlights:

  • Authentic Cuisine: Focuses on Kerala's treasured, age-old recipes, such as Appam with Stew, Mutton Mappas, and various seafood dishes.
  • Atmosphere: A modern, casual dining setting overlooking the serene backwaters.
  • Dining Options: Offers an all-day menu, including an extensive breakfast buffet and a renowned Sunday brunch.
  • Service: Highly rated for its warm, attentive service and hospitality.
  • Location: Situated within the Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatty, offering indoor and outdoor seating

Our Story & Philosophy

The restaurant's concept is built around preserving the traditional tastes of the region while presenting them in a relaxed, contemporary setting.

  • Lady Masterchefs: The kitchen is notably led by Chef Latha, who, along with her team, travelled across Kerala to discover and document lesser-known, traditional recipes from older generations.
  • Heritage-Driven: The menu draws inspiration from the city's cultural legacies, specifically focusing on the aromatic and spicy flavors typical of the Malabar coast.
  • Freshness & Quality: A core tenet of the café is the use of the freshest locally sourced ingredients, from seafood to seasonal vegetables.

Culinary Highlights

  • Authentic Kerala Cuisine:: Specializes in regional favorites like Kerala Beef Fry, Meen Manga Thakali (mango fish curry), and traditional Appams made in cast-iron woks.
  • Daily Buffet: Offers an extensive [buffet breakfast](https://www.hyatt diningclub.com) and lunch featuring local dishes, live counters for Puttu and Dosas, and a hyper-traditional "Pazham Kanji" (rice gruel).
  • Thali Experience:Features "The Local Market Thali," which provides a curated sampling of various Kerala flavors
