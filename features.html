Key Features of Malabar Cafe

Where Taste Meets Ambiance • premier dining destination focusing on authentic Kerala cuisine, specifically featuring Malabar spices

  • Culinary Focus: Known for authentic Kerala cuisine, including specialties like duck mappas and Kerala porotta, with dishes reflecting Arab, Brahmin, and Zamorin influences.
  • Dining Atmosphere: A modern yet relaxed setting with both indoor and outdoor seating options.
  • Location: Situated within the 5-star Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatty, offering a luxurious setting on Bolgatty Island.
  • Meal Service & Accessibility: Offers breakfast, lunch, and dinner with accessibility features such as valet parking, wheelchair-accessible entrances, and high chairs for children.
  • Unique Features: The cafe is known for its great coffee, desserts, and in some cases, live music. It is important to note that the Malabar Cafe does not serve alcohol.
